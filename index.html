Who is a better influence, Kanye or Jay-Z?
Maybe you were always a fan of Etta James.
Maybe you love electronic and first fell in love with her thanks to the earworm hook from “Something’s Got a Hold on Me” that Pretty Lights sampled in the 2006 track “Finally Moving”?
Or maybe you’re more of a hip hop fan and didn’t hear that hook until Flo Rida used it five years later for “Good Feeling”?
Or maybe you still have no idea what I’m on about.
But one thing is for sure - The art of sampling, remixing, and covering - essentially relying on original recordings from yonder to enhance your music has become standard procedure. Which raises the question - what is the impact for the original artists that are sampled? Do they see an increase or resurgence in popularity, and how can we measure that?
